हमारा संकल्प - AI Janta Party Manifesto

01. Corrupt netao ko nikalo.
02. Nikammi aur corrupt police ko nikalo aur berozgaaro ko mauka do. Berojgaron ki kami nahi.
03. Logo ko rojgaar do, pehle se maujood corrupt officers ko nikaalo berozgaar ko mauka do.
04. Homeschooling ko legally vyavasthit karo jisse lutere school clg pe laagaam laegi aur sarkari education ka kharcha bhi kam hoga.
05. Logo ka ghar thodne ki jagah jisne unhe jameen bechi unka ghar toda jaana chahiye jisse Bhumafia niyantrit hoga.
06. Koi bhi case 1 saal se jada samay tak Pending nahi rah sakti anyatha judge ko fasi hogi.
07. Mahila aur purush ke gender neutral law honge.
08. Nagar ki saaf saafai ka dekh rekh na karne pe nagar nigam ke adhikariyo ko 7 saal ki saja hogi bina bail ke.
09. Madir, masjid, jaat paat ko reconstruction kiya jayega jisse ucch nich ka antar khatam ho.
10. Mahilao ko angpradarsan karne pe 2 saal ki jail hogi, apki body koi content nahi hai iska sammaan kare aur samaaj me apni bhumika ko majboot kare.
11. Railway ki vyavastha ko sudhara jayega aur kisi bhi tarah ki manmaani karne pe adhikaari ko hamesa ke liye suspend kar diya aur uska certificate license bhi radd kar diya jayega.
12. Hospital ke doctors ki manmaani khatam ki jaayegi aur shikaayat dharj hone par permanent license radd kiya jayega.
13. Homeschooling ke saath hi government skill center honge jaha log apni ikcha se skill sikh sakenge.
14. Import ko kam kiya jayega export ko badhaya jayega.
15. Paper leak karne wale adhikariyon ko faasi kyuki paper leak hone pe naa jaane kitne bacche isi faasi pe jhul jaaate hain
